My first day in a tech school

June 4,2024

Excitement and Nerves

I woke up early,feeling a mix of Excitement and nerves.Today was my first day at SQI a tech school,and i could not wait to start learning new skills and meeting new people.

Orientation

The day started with an Orientation session,where we were introduced to the school faculty and staff. We also got a tour of the campus and learned about the school policies and procedures.

Meeting New People

After Orientation,we had a chance to met our fellow students. I was relieved to find that everyone was friendly and eager to learn. We exchanged introductions and started forming connections.

First class

The first class was an introduction to HTML.I was excited to dive into the world of coding and learn the basics of web development.The instructor was knowledgeable and made the subject engaging.

Conclusion

Overall,my first day at tech school was a positive experience. I am looking forward to learn new skills,making new friends,and growing as a developer.
